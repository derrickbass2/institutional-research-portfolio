# Data Dictionary

Project: Institutional Characteristics and Student Success Outcomes in California Community Colleges (2019–2023)

---

## Dependent Variable

### Fall-to-Fall Persistence Rate

Definition: Percentage of first-time students in a fall term who re-enroll in the subsequent fall term.
Source: California Community Colleges Data Mart
Unit of Analysis: Institutional-level percentage
Time Frame: 2019–2023

---

## Independent Variables

### Percentage of Pell Recipients

Definition: Proportion of enrolled students receiving Pell Grant funding.
Source: CCCCO Data Mart
Unit: Percentage

### Enrollment Size

Definition: Total headcount enrollment.
Source: CCCCO Data Mart
Unit: Count

### Percentage Part-Time Students

Definition: Proportion of enrolled students classified as part-time.
Source: CCCCO Data Mart
Unit: Percentage

### Student-Faculty Ratio

Definition: Ratio of enrolled students to full-time equivalent faculty.
Source: CCCCO Data Mart
Unit: Ratio

### Full-Time Equivalent (FTE) Faculty

Definition: Total instructional faculty expressed as FTE.
Source: CCCCO Data Mart
Unit: FTE count

---

## Notes on Data Integrity

- All variables will be aggregated at the institutional level.
- Multi-year averages (2019–2023) will be calculated to reduce year-specific volatility.
- Missing values will be documented prior to model specification.
